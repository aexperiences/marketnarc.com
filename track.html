How I score, in plain English. A Buy is a hit if the stock is up 4%+ within 30 trading days; a Sell if it's down 4%+; a Hold if it stays within ±4% (if it breaks out, that's a miss — I don't hide from it). A Watch never counts as a win or a loss — it's me choosing not to call it. The rule is set before the call, not after. Prices via the live market feed; every call is timestamped and logged the moment it's made.
